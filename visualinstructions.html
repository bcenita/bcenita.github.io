Visual Communication Design: How to Squat

Timeline: Feb 2017 - Mar 2017

Role: Graphic Designer

Tools: Adobe Illustrator

Skills: Storyboarding, Iteration, Design Critiquing, Usability Testing, Graphic Design

Description: Designed a step by step procedure for performing the barbell back squat in vector graphics.

Key Takeaways:

  • Proficiency in Adobe Illustrator
  • Getting my work critiqued, and iterating on feedback
  • Fundamentals of creating vector graphics
  • Preparing vector graphics for web (SVGs and HTML)
Final Vector Graphic Procedure
Final Photo Procedure

Context

In a course focused on Visual Communication, I was tasked to pick a procedure of my choosing and communicate it as a series of steps through both photos and vector graphics.

Design Process:

  1. Storyboarding
  2. Initial Photo Sequence
  3. Initial Graphics Sequence
    • From our design critique, I learned that the arrows I used to indicate motion were not very intuitive

  4. Usability Test and Findings
